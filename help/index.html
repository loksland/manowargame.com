Frequently Asked Questions

Q. I’m stuck on a level
If the level is in the first few stages you can pause and select skip to move on and come back to it later. If you’re really stuck tweet @manowargame for a hint.

Q. Where is the bonus stage?
The submarine stage is hidden. You have to go hunting for it. Don’t give up! If you can’t find it email us at help@manowargame.com for a clue.

Q. Where’s the Android version?
I would love to be on Android one day, though due to the resource limitations of being a one-man independant release I can only focus on iOS for the first release of this game.
