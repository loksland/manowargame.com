{% markdown %} ####Frequently Asked Questions#### Q. **I'm stuck on a level** If the level is in the first few stages you can pause and select skip to move on and come back to it later. If you're really stuck tweet [@manowargame](https://twitter.com/manowargame) for a hint. Q. **Where is the bonus stage?** The submarine stage is hidden. You have to go hunting for it. Don't give up! If you can't find it email us at help@manowargame.com for a clue. Q. **Where's the Android version?** I would love to be on Android one day, though due to the resource limitations of being a one-man independant release I can only focus on iOS for the first release of this game. {% comment %} Q. **Will there be an Android version?** An Android version is not currently planned. Q. **My husband has been offered a job out of state. My whole family is in the Delaware area. What do I do?** Tough call. I would recommend talking to him openly about your fears of being isolated in a new city. Tasha is at a good age to move and kids are more resilient to change than you can imagine.
