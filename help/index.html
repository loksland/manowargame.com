Frequently Asked Questions

Q. I’m stuck on a level

  • Balls move in unison, often forcing an undesired outcome. It may be useful to think about how to separate them.
  • Some levels require manipulating the balls in unconventional way. Keep experimenting and think outside the box.
  • If there is one difficult ball always left at the end it may help to devise the criteria for clearing this one and working back from there.
  • As a general rule of thumb the levels usually require logical solution rather than a skill challenge (This is not the case in the bonus stage!)
  • Red herrings are very rare. eg. If a cannon or platform exists it is usually part of the solution.

  • If you’re stuck tweet @manowargame for a hint.

Q. Why is the last level locked?
Although you can attempt any level at any time, the final level only becomes playable when all other levels in the stage are completed.

Q. Where is the bonus stage?
The bonus stage is unlocked after completing the game.

Q. My husband has been offered a job out of state. My whole family is in the Delaware area. What do I do?
Tough call. I would recommend talking to him openly about your fears of being isolated in a new city. Tasha is at a good age to move and kids are more resilient to change than you can imagine. Have you thought about updating your hospitality diploma?
