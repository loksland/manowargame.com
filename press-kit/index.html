Manowar: what doesn't kill you will make you ponder {% markdown %} Journey over 60 individually crafted levels that each pose a unique challenge to be unraveled

  • pay once for the whole experience: no IAP or ads
  • universal app for iPad and iPhone
  • unlock the speed run mode, compete on the global leaderboards, challenge friends with game center achievements
How to play
  • tilt the ship and tap to fire cannons, empty the ship to solve the puzzle
  • designed to simultaneously challenge your logic, lateral thinking and timing skills
