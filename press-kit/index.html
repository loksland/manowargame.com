Description

Manowar is a physics puzzle game set on a warship on the dark seas. With fates linked, work together to unlock your freedom.

TLDR: You tilt a boat and fire cannons

Features

  • The mechanic is new
  • Pay Once and Play. No in-app-purchases
  • Hours of lovingly designed levels over 4 stages
  • The music is sublime
  • Playable with one-hand for all the people holding a beer or a baby out there
  • You can listen to podcasts / iTunes while playing
  • Secret bonus stage on a submarine. You heard me.
