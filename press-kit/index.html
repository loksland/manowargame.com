Description

{% markdown %} **Manowar** is a physics puzzle game set on a warship on the dark seas. With fates linked, work together to unlock your freedom. {% comment %} In the depths of a ferocious storm in foreign seas, an infamous crew of pirates came across a mysterious golden vessel alone and in distress. A sitting duck! When they attempted to swoop in and plunder the seemingly defenseless ship a powerful supernatural force overcame them at once, twisting their limbs, burning their eyes and melting the ground where they stood. When they awoke their human form had long fled, transformed into that of leaden cannonballs. Their once loved vessel was now an elaborate prison. Cursed to the drift the ancient seas for all of eternity, these ancient warriors once again seek out the mysterious vessel that cursed their fates so many lifetimes ago. {% endcomment %} TLDR: You tilt a boat and fire cannons ####Features#### * The mechanic is new * Pay Once and Play. No in-app-purchases * Hours of lovingly designed levels over 4 stages * The music, composed by Keir Nuttall, is sublime * Playable with one-hand for all the people holding a beer or a baby out there * You can listen to podcasts / iTunes while playing * Secret bonus stage on a submarine. You heard me. {% endmarkdown %}
